JEWELRY✰



HEART EARRINGS

05/10/2023

I made these earrings for a special teacher before my high school graduation. She had done a lot for me and my friends throughout highschool, so I wanted to gove her something special and personal, so I designed these based on her style. She loves to wear big jewelry, and so I felt that these were perfect. I gave her the blue pair, and also made a white pair for my mom since she liked the design as well.

VALENTINES BRACELETS

02/14/2023

I was an executive for the an envionmental club in my high school, and created a bunch of valentines day themed bracelets to sell during a bake sale fundraiser for WWF. These were fun especially since I had to come up with multiple different types of designs along with a deadline. They all sold out, plus, I recieved many custom orders afterward!

CLOVER CHOKER

05/19/2023

I made this choker necklace as a gift for my friend's birthday. This style of jewelry making, with the linked beads, is my favorite, and therapeutic to put together. I chose the beads based on her favorite colors and knowing that the shades and style would match her outfits as well.

BRAIDED RINGS

06/05/2022

These braided rings were some of the first pieces of jewelry I made when I started practicing this hobby, and also became my most popular item sold through my small business. I've lost count of how many I've made, but i've improved each time, and love trying out different color combinations with gold and silver wire and different types of shades of beads.








JOURNALING✰




PEACH PIT

01/07/2024

After starting college, it's been tough to find time to journal, so I did as much as I could over winter break. I made this spread wanting to play with cutout letters I made, and based them off of song lyrics from a song I'd been listening to (Peach Pit by Peach Pit). I was also able to mix some things I collected both in college station and high school (ex. the receipt is from cstat and the scorpion logo is from my high school).

PROM NIGHT

07/03/2023

I made this spread based off of the photo which was taken at my prom. I love to create ramdom mix match spreads, but this time, I had a focused color palette and theme. I incorporated the ticket to the event and chose scotch tape that seemed on theme (it literally says Celebrate!).

INK DOODLES

03/13/2023

I wanted to experiment with ink doodles and incorporate that into my journaling style, and included song lyrics from a song whose vibe I think matched the doodles (Windmill by McAfferty). It was fun challenging myself to come up with ways to fill the space and not being as minimalistic as I tended to be with my spreads before this!

LIGHT MY F(EYE)IRE

02/28/2023

Something I tend to do with art sometimes is limit my pallette, but with this one, I wanted to play with my colors, and it was so much fun. I mixed numerous shades of colored pencils to draw the eyes, and continued the doodles and design around them. I incorporated song lyrics from one of my favorite songs from my favorite bands (Move Me by half•alive).

MY FAVORITE

01/23/2023

Not only is this spread my personal favorite, but I also believe it matches my style and taste the most. It features stars and butterflies, some of my favorite elements, and a color theme that I love. I love including receipts and book pages as well, both of which are featured here. This was also one of the first times I wasn't afraid to just use the gluestick and see what happens, and I'm proud of how it turned out. I no longer hesitate with my art.








PHOTOGRAPHY✰




TWO FACED

11/22/2022

For my photography class, this photo was taken to demonstrate what slow shutter speed can do, and to convery a dark and unsettling tone. It took many attempts, but I kept trying till I was satisfied. I put a green sticky note over my phone flashlight, and moved my head within 5 seconds.

DECEMBER DUNK

12/08/2022

I was the media director for a couple of clubs in high school, so I got the ooportunity to take pictures at numerous events, pictures that I'd post on the club social media, and share with the magazine and yearbook teams for our school. This basketball fundraiser event was hosted by STUCO and as media director/photographer, I got to practice taking action shots. This one is my favorite, moments before a dunk!

ANGLES

01/11/2024

I had my friends pose in front of a white sheet backdrop with newspapers arrounding them and pops of color through their lipstick. Color scheme, facial expression, and the angle at which the photo was taken were all considered to create an editorial style result. I first became interested in this portrait style when I came accross work by Michela Riva, who does odd fashion photography. I also applied to be a photographer for TAMU's Aline Magazine and gained inspiration from them as well.
